## Weekly theme (the one big thing on top of the Daily 3)
| Day | Theme |
|-----|-------|
| Mon | Plan & load — build week's lead list (50–75), review last week's numbers |
| Tue | Content — batch-film/edit 3–5 clips |
| Wed | **Wedge day** — produce 2–3 free sample edits / teardowns (highest leverage) |
| Thu | Sales — push proposals/follow-ups, discovery calls, ask for referrals |
| Fri | Proof & polish — turn a win into a case study + post, update portfolio |
| **Fri+Sat** | 🌴 **Off days** (game auto-off; light engagement only if you want) |

**Why outreach is evening, not morning:** BD 6–9 PM = US Eastern 8–11 AM — that's when prospects are online, so DMs/posts land. Mornings/afternoons need no one online → deep work (client edits, content batching, sample edits).

**The 2-minute rule:** low day? Open the tracker, send ONE DM. Momentum does the rest. Inputs over feelings.

## 2. MARKET SCAN — what top apps do (and what's worth borrowing)

Researched the leading gamified/habit apps. Key takeaways:

- **Habitica** — RPG avatar, XP, gold, collectible pets, parties & quests *with friends*. The "0.5-second dopamine hit" after each check is what beats a plain checkmark. → *Borrow: a reward shop + optional accountability buddy.*
- **Finch** — gentle self-care pet; **missed days never punish you**, the bird just waits. Mood check-ins. → *Borrow: mood tracking + your already-good gentle Rest Day framing.*
- **Routinery** — a **timed "start routine" mode** that walks you block-by-block with a countdown. → *Borrow: a per-block focus timer (this directly boosts deep-work quality).*
- **Streaks** — deliberately **simple**, few habits, health integration. → *Lesson: don't over-feature; simplicity sustains.*

### Feature shortlist (ONLY after the business is moving — not now)
1. ⏱️ **Per-block focus timer** (Pomodoro/countdown) — best ROI, improves the actual work
2. 🎁 **Reward shop** — spend XP on real-life rewards (sustains motivation long-term)
3. 📊 **Trends/charts** — see which stats climb over weeks
4. 😌 **Mood + 1-line reflection** at shutdown
5. 🤝 **Accountability** — weekly auto-export of your scoreboard to a mentor/friend
6. 🔔 **PWA reminders** at block times

> Note: every one of these is optional polish. None signs a client. Build at most ONE per month, and only as a reward for hitting outreach quotas.

## 3. MAKING EACH PILLAR MORE EFFECTIVE (the research-backed upgrades)

### 🎯 Deep Work / Time Blocks
- **60–90 min sprints.** Research: it takes 40–50 min to reach deep focus; 60–90 min unbroken delivers the best results. Split your multi-hour Deep Work into 2 sprints with a real break.
- **15–30 min buffer** around focus blocks for recovery and overflow.
- **Energy management** — hardest task (sales/creative) at your peak; admin in the slump.
- **Hard stop + no weekend grind** — protects against burnout (Newport). You already do this via Rest Day.
- **Day theming** — e.g., Mon = outreach blitz, Tue/Thu = client delivery, Wed = content, Fri = skill deep-dive.

### 🧠 Skill Learning (stop dabbling — go deliberate)
- **The 20-hour rule:** functional competency in a skill ≈ 20 focused hours *if you practice the highest-impact sub-skills first.*
- **Deliberate practice:** pick ONE narrow sub-skill, work just beyond your ability, get **real feedback** (post it, ask a pro, compare to a reference), repeat.
- **Spaced repetition:** short daily sessions beat marathon cramming.
- **PRIORITIZE — don't run all 7 skills equally.** For a $5M agency, rank:
  1. **Sales & Marketing** (gets clients — your #1 gap)
  2. **Communication** (closes calls, runs a team)
  3. **AI** (10x multiplier on your craft + speed)
  4. Motion / Editing / Content — you have 7 years; *maintain*, don't over-invest
  5. Future-thinking — emerges naturally from the above
- Rotate the *star* skill of the day, but spend the real depth on #1–#3 for the next 6 months.

### 📢 Content Creation / Personal Brand
- **Consistency > frequency.** Posting weekly for 6 months beats daily for 1 month then quitting. Pick a cadence you can *sustain* (3×/week is plenty).
- **Reposition.** "Video editor / content creator" → price-shoppers. "I help B2B brands get leads & authority with video" → serious clients.
- **Signature aesthetic.** One recognizable style (your color grade, your hook rhythm) builds recall → trust.
- **Living portfolio.** Not a link page — a feed of recent work, breakdowns, case-study snippets, BTS. Market *by creating*.
- **Timeline:** early inquiries usually 2–3 months in; sustainable client flow 6–9 months. So **start posting this week** — the clock starts when you do.
